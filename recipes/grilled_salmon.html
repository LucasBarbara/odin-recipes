Grilled Salmon

Description

The simplest things of all are sometimes the tastier. This recipe of grilled salmon pairs perfectly with grilled vegetables, asparagus or quinoa. Quick, easy and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 400 g of fresh salmon
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • salt to taste
  • black pepper to taste
  • juice of half a lemon
  1. Season the salmon fillets with salt, pepper and half of the olive oil. Let it rest for 15 minutes.
  2. Add the rest of the olive oil to a skillet. Add the salmon when hot and let it cook for a couple of minutes on each size.
  3. When cooked to taste, turn off the heat and add the lemon juice.
  4. Serve with your favorite side dish.
