Vegetarian Chili

Description

This vegetarian chili is spicy, smoky and comforting. Everything a chili should be. Tasty and heathy, it is a great recipe for a winter night.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium onion
  • 2 large green bell peppers
  • 1 large carrot
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 4 large tomatoes
  • 1 large can of pinto beans
  • 2 large can of kidney beans
  • 1 bunch of fresh cilantro
  • 2 tbs of olive oil
  • salt to taste
  • 2 teaspoon of cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • chili powder to taste
  • 2 cupas of vegetable broth
  • avocado slices and sour cream to top up
  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the chopped onion, garlic and bell pepper. Cook for about 10 minutes or until the vegetableas are cooked and translucent.
  2. Add the grated carrot and spices and cook for another 3 minutes.
  3. Add the chopped tomatoes and their juices to the pan. Cook for another minute, stirring. Then add the beans and the broth. Let it simmer and reduce the heat as necessary to slowly cook for 35 minutes.
  4. Taste for salt and add as necessary. Stir, and then add the chopped cilantro. Serve in bowls and garnish with the sliced avocado and sour cream to taste.
